Who we are...

Fast Twitch Fitness is a specialized sports gym in Appleton, WI. FTF's goal is to help athletes exceed their athletic goals and prepare them for competition. FTF will help you break down your goals and assign you to a trainer from their diverse group of personal trainers. FTF and their trainers will provide you with the necessary training and meal plan to be the best athlete you can be.
